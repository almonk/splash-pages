Account Manager

GoCardless is one of London’s fastest growing startups. We serve thousands of customers, with hundreds more being added each month.

Our Account Management team is new, meaning this is an exciting opportunity to help shape the function. You will be helping us understand our customers, uncover new business opportunities and ensuring our service is better than anyone else. We are currently working with a range of businesses including large newspaper groups, crowdfunding services and SaaS companies.

Every day you will be:

  • Building strong, long-term relationships with merchants
  • Helping merchants switch their customers over to GoCardless
  • Proactively uncovering new business opportunities

There’s very little hierarchy, a common hunger to learn, lots of freedom and all the responsibility you want to take. There’s also a great office atmosphere, good coffee, tons of fruit and juice, Friday drinks and regular dinners out.

The competencies you need to be great at this role:

  • Integrity and conscientiousness
  • Proactive and persistent – go the extra mile for our customers even if it won’t result in immediate measurable gain
  • Organised, efficient and ability to deal with detail
  • Strong communication skills

Bonus points for previous experience in account management.

Base salary of £20-30k (negotiable depending on experience), plus uncapped performance-related bonuses.
