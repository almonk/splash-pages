Head of Marketing

Establish and promote the GoCardless brand to help drive our growth.

We’re an ambitious high-growth company that is quickly evolving. Since this time last year, our revenue has grown 450% and our team has more than doubled in size. As part of our strategy for continued rapid growth, we’re expanding our operations internationally and working with increasingly large organisations. We are looking for a Head of Marketing to make sure that the businesses that would benefit from our product know about us.

About the role

As Head of Marketing, you will be responsible for raising awareness, establishing a strong, coherent GoCardless brand and driving the growth we need to become the dominant recurring payments provider in Europe within the next couple of years.

You'll set the direction for the marketing team, and ensure the team (and yourself) are focused on the right activities to make sure the people who need us know about us. You'll need to become an expert in our growth and customer acquisition metrics and in measuring performance.

You will work closely with the founders of the company as well as interacting with every part of the GoCardless organisation from engineering to sales to ensure we're doing everything we can to keep driving strong growth.

Requirements:

  • Expert knowledge of organic and paid marketing techniques
  • Experience running marketing at a fast-growing technology company
  • Strong technical awareness

Industry competitive salary. This role would ideally suit someone with experience marketing a fast-growing technology company.
