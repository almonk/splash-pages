Head of Marketing

Establish and promote the GoCardless brand to help drive our growth.

We’re an ambitious high-growth company that is quickly evolving. Since this time last year, our revenue has grown 450% and our team has more than doubled in size. We are looking for a Head of Marketing to drive our growth by building a strong brand and ensuring that we reach the right people through the right means.

About the role

As Head of Marketing, you will be instrumental in establishing GoCardless as the leading recurring payments provider in Europe. You’ll be responsible for driving growth by:

  • crafting a coherent brand
  • ensuring we tell a compelling story
  • building our voice in the marketplace
  • becoming expert in metrics for growth, customer acquisition, and measuring our performance

You’ll work closely with the founders of the company to set the direction for the marketing team, and you’ll be responsible for keeping the team (and yourself) focused on the right activities to make sure that people who need us know about us.

You’ll collaborate with the design team and you’ll interact with every part of the organisation from engineering to sales to ensure that we’re doing everything we can to build our brand and sustain our awesome rate of growth.

We’re looking for someone with:

  • A deep understanding of social media, organic and paid marketing techniques
  • Experience creating or commissioning creative advertising
  • Experience building brands that stand for something in the world
  • A strong point of view on the future of marketing
  • Strong technical awareness

Industry competitive salary. This role would ideally suit someone with experience marketing a fast-growing technology company.
