Introducing super simple online payments

GoCardless Basic is the quickest and easiest way to take one-off and recurring payments online. Our simple dashboard lets you collect payments directly from customers' bank accounts, saving you time and money.

Automate everything

Once authorised, all payments will be taken from your customer’s bank account without any intervention needed.

High customer retention

With failure rates of just 0.5% GoCardless means not losing customers through card expiry.

No hidden costs

GoCardless Basic costs just 1% per transaction (capped at £2) with no monthly or set up fees. Scale pricing is also available.

Get started in minutes

All you need to get started is a UK bank account. Sign up now and you’ll be taking payments in minutes.

Military-grade security

We store all bank details with RSA encryption and communicate only over secure channels.

Real-time payment tracking

Get live updates on the status of your pending payments. We’ll notify you if payments fail or are cancelled.
