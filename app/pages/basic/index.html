Introducing super simple online payments

GoCardless Basic is the quickest and easiest way to take one-off and recurring payments online. Our simple dashboard lets you collect payments directly from customers' bank accounts, saving you time and money.

Get started in minutes

All you need to get started is a UK bank account. Sign up now and you could be taking payments in minutes.

No setup fees

GoCardless Basic costs just 1% per transaction (capped at £2) with no monthly or setup fees. Scale pricing is also available.

Automate everything

Once authorised, all payments will be taken from your customer’s bank account without them needing to do anything further.

High customer retention

Because it's built on the Direct Debit system, GoCardless means not losing customers through card expiry or cancellation.

Military-grade security

We store all bank details with RSA encryption and communicate only over secure channels.

Real-time payment tracking

Get live updates on the status of your pending payments. We’ll instantly notify you if payments fail or are cancelled.
