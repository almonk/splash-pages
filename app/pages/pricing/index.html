Are there really no other fees?

No, there are no monthly charges or hidden fees of any kind. All you pay is 1% of the fee on a transaction, up to a maximum of £2. This makes us much cheaper than cards, or other services like Paypal.

Do you offer discounts large volume?

Yes. You are eligible if you take more than 500 payments per month, read more here. If you take thousands of payments per month, it makes sense to consider GoCardless Pro.

What does ‘capped at £2’ mean?

You will never pay more than £2 per transaction. If your transaction is for £199, the fee is £1.99. If the transaction value is anything greater than £200, the fee is £2.00.

Will I still be charged if a payment fails?

No. Our standard fee structure — 1%, capped at £2 — is all you will ever pay.

There is no set up fee. GoCardless also offers a host of free tools to help speed up your integration.

Is there a fee if a customer makes a chargeback?
