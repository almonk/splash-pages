Support Analyst

Solve our users’ problems and help us make something people want.

GoCardless is one of London’s most exciting tech start-ups. We’re building the next generation of online payments and already provide simple Direct Debit to thousands of small and medium sized businesses.

We pride ourselves on giving our users first-rate support and going the extra mile to keep them happy. We are growing fast and looking for someone versatile, smart and enthusiastic to join our support team.

Every day you will be:

  • Working as part of a close-knit team
  • Solving problems and answering questions by email, phone and chat
  • Working with our product team to ensure those problems don’t arise again
  • Identifying high-value prospective users and ensuring the sales team follow up
  • Improving someone’s day

You’ll have the chance to help us develop our processes to identify fraudulent payments & users. There is also the opportunity to delve into the product on a technical level, which includes expanding your own technical ability as you tackle customer integration issues.

You will work with an incredibly talented team and have opportunities to learn from them. There’s a great office atmosphere, good coffee, tons of fruit and juice, Friday beers and regular dinners out.

We’re looking for someone who:

  • Has heaps of initiative, enthusiasm and patience
  • Can multitask effortlessly
  • Has a flawless command of English
  • Is technically literate and keen to learn

Bonus points for previous experience at a start-up and/or in customer support.
