Junior Sales Executive

Play a key role in growing one of London’s most exciting tech start-ups.

GoCardless is growing fast and we are looking for smart, driven salespeople to join our high performing sales team and continue to accelerate our growth.

Every day you will be:

  • Working as part of a driven, competitive (but close-knit!) team selling a truly exceptional product
  • Talking to prospective customers in order to understand their needs
  • Executing our sales process & closing deals

You will work with an incredibly talented team and have opportunities to learn from them – you’ll work closely with one of the founders and share sales techniques with the existing team.

There’s very little hierarchy, a common hunger to learn, lots of freedom and all the responsibility you want to take. There’s also a great office atmosphere, good coffee, tons of fruit and juice, Friday beers and regular dinners out.

The skills you need to be great at this role:

  • Drive, persistence and competitiveness
  • Organisation and ability to deal with detail
  • Coachability and desire to learn

Bonus points for:

  • Previous experience in sales or the payments industry.

Competitive base salary (negotiable depending on experience), plus uncapped performance-related bonuses.
