Web Designer

At GoCardless, we care deeply about creating great experiences for our customers. We are looking for talented web designers to help us craft these experiences.

We believe in an iterative design process, and work hand-in-hand with our frontend developers to implement our designs. You will initially be working closely with our marketing team to improve the experience for prospective GoCardless customers.

On a day-to-day basis, you will be:

  • Working with our marketing team to design beautiful splash pages for our website
  • Iterating on our communication to make sure we're always doing the best job of selling GoCardless
  • Designing new flows in our product to delight our users

You should apply if you:

  • Have spectacular visual design skills — obsessing over simplicity, clarity and pixel perfection
  • Can demonstrate your amazing communication skills
  • Are excited by a startup atmosphere and the challenge to help to grow our brand

You'd be a particularly good fit if you:

  • Knowledge of HTML, CSS & Javascript
  • Have previously worked with (or within) engineering and marketing teams
  • Have experience working in an agency or as a freelancer

There’s very little hierarchy, a common hunger to learn, lots of freedom and all the responsibility you want to take. There’s also a great office atmosphere, good coffee, tons of fruit and juice, Friday beers and regular dinners out.
