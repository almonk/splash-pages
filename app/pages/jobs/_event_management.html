Event Management

We are looking for someone smart, organised and effective to help work out which events & conferences the team should attend and to organise our attendance to maximise results.

As the team grows, there will be scope to develop & expand your role to run what we hope will be a growing channel.

On a day-to-day basis, you will be:

  • Analysing our existing high-value clients to identify & rank which events we should target
  • Organising every aspect of our attendance from initial registration to post-event follow-up
  • Making sure that everything is tracked so that we can take the things that work and turn them into repeatable processes

Requirements:

  • Organised with fantastic attention to detail
  • Smart, results oriented with a “doer” mindset
  • Strong work ethic, drive and desire to learn

Bonus points for:

  • Tech savvy

Salary in the range of £20-30,000 per year. This role would ideally suit a recent graduate with 0-3 years’ professional experience
