Swish of the Curtain halved their payment collection time by switching to GoCardless

“Switching to GoCardless has halved the time we spend on reconciling and collecting payments”

Chris Witham, Manager of Swish of the Curtain

GoCardless helped them…

  • Halve the time they spend reconciling and collecting payments
  • Move to the paperless model they were looking for
  • Save up to a week of admin time every month

Challenge

“We were spending a week a month physically sending out, reconciling and chasing payments”

Swish of the Curtain is a theatre company which teaches children from 2 - 20 in the performing arts. Before GoCardless, Swish of the Curtain were using continuous authorisation and sending out monthly invoices. “We had difficulties with reconciliation and we were spending a week a month physically sending out, reconciling and chasing payments”.

Solution

“GoCardless seemed to be a simple and affordable solution”

Swish of the Curtain started looking at managing Direct Debits in-house but were, “put off by the time and maintenance involved in keeping on top of new legislation”. They started looking for a more mobile solution and discovered GoCardless through Kashflow. “GoCardless and Kashflow seemed to be a simple and affordable solution to the dilemma of managing lots of small payments”.

Benefits

“Switching to GoCardless has halved the time we spend on reconciling and collecting payments”

Using GoCardless, Swish of the Curtain have been able to move towards a paperless model. “Switching to GoCardless has halved the time we spend on reconciling and collecting payments and allows us to dedicate our time to improving our service for existing customers and recruiting new customers.”
