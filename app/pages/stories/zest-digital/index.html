“Chasing payments was a waste of time”

Zest Digital is a digital marketing agency based in Oxfordshire. By automating processes wherever possible, the company has served hundreds of clients with just a handful of staff. A year ago, MD Alex Minchin realised that an overhaul of their payment process was needed to help them grow.

"We used to get paid by manual bank transfers but managing invoices and chasing payments was a waste of time," says Alex. With average debtor days of 76 days, Zest's growth was stifled by poor cashflow and they were unable to hire new staff despite a growing order book.

“GoCardless were by far the best”

Alex believed that Direct Debit was the most reliable solution and after considering several Direct Debit providers, selected GoCardless. "I spoke with a number of Direct Debit providers and GoCardless were by far the best. All the others had large up-front fees and seemed much more difficult to integrate."
