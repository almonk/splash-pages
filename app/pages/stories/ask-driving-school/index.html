Ask Driving School reduced accounting time by 80% after switching to Direct Debit with GoCardless

“Using GoCardless transformed our cash flow.”

Carl Mainwaring, Ask Driving School

GoCardless helped them…

  • Reduce accounting time by 80%
  • Eliminate cash flow issues
  • Grow 200% year on year

Challenge

“Payments were often in arrears for months”

Ask Driving School is one of the UK's largest franchise driving schools, helping UK instructors find and teach new students.

"We invoiced instructors on a monthly basis but payments were often in arrears for months. Managing the company's accounts and payments grew to be a full time job," says Director Carl Mainwaring. This uncertainty in their cash flow was holding back the company's growth.

Solution

“GoCardless and Kashflow do all the hard work for us”

Ask started using GoCardless through online accounting platform KashFlow. "GoCardless and KashFlow do all the hard work for us. We issue recurring invoices in Kashflow, and each instructor sets up a variable Direct Debit with GoCardless.” With GoCardless integrated into their accounting software, payments reconcile automatically.

Benefits

“Every payment is collected automatically and on time”

Since turning to GoCardless, “every payment is collected automatically and on time.” As a result, Ask has reduced the time spent on accounting by 80% and significantly improved cashflow. Ask can now focus their energy on growing the business.

Within a year of joining GoCardless, Ask Driving School has more than doubled in size. “Using GoCardless has transformed our cash flow, and our prospects.”
