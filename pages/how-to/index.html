Getting started with GoCardless

GoCardless is the simplest way to collect payment by Direct Debit.
Here's how to get started.

  • Set up a customer

  • Take a payment

  • Bill multiple customers

  • View your payments

  • Customise your branding

  • Remind pending customers

  • Receive your money

  • Great customer support

Set up a customer

To collect Direct Debit payments you need your customer to authorise you first. With GoCardless the whole process is managed online.

To ask for authorisation, first navigate to the 'Customers' tab:

Next, click 'Add':

Your customer will receive an email linking them to the GoCardless payment pages to set up their Direct Debit. We'll notify you as soon as they do, and you will then be able to add payments for them.

As well as adding customers individually, you can also:

  • Add multiple customers at once using a .csv file
  • Add customers from a link placed in an email or on your website

Take a payment

First make sure you've added the customer you want to bill.

To take payment from a customer, first navigate to the 'Customers' tab:

Then select the customer you want to bill:

Next, click 'Take Payment' and enter details of the payment you'd like to collect:

This may be a one-off payment, or a recurring one. Alternatively, you can add the customer to a plan you've already created.

GoCardless will notify your customer about the payments you've set up and start collecting the payment. Because you're collecting by Direct Debit, this can take up to 5 working days.

Bill multiple customers

If you have a lot of customers on the same payment schedule you can create a single payment plan for all those customers. Once created, you can add new and existing customers to the plan manually, or use the plan's 'PayLink' to let customers sign up from your website or from an email.

You'll also be able to edit the plan in future if your prices change.

To create a new plan, navigate to the 'Plans' tab:

Click 'Create Plan' and enter details of the payment plan you'd like to set up:

Once set up, your plan will appear in your 'Plans' tab. Clicking on it will give you more details, including the customers enrolled on it.

Adding customers to your plan

To add new or existing customers to your payment plan, first navigate to the 'Customers' tab:

For a new customer, click "Add customer" and select "Add to payment plan" in the following dialog:

As soon as your customer has authorized a Direct Debit to you, we'll add them to your payment plan.

The process for existing customers is similar - just choose their name from the 'Customers' screen, click "Take Payment" and then select the "Add to payment plan" option.

If a customer is on a plan, you can still take ad-hoc payments from them by choosing them from the 'Customers' screen and then clicking 'Take Payment'.

Customise your branding

Within "Settings" you can customise your account in two key ways:

  1. Add your company logo to the GoCardless payment pages
  2. Specify how you want payments to appear on your customers' bank statements

Both of these options are available in the 'Business Details' section. You can find this by clicking the cog in the top right hand corner of the screen and selecting 'Settings'.

Reminding pending users

You can see which customers have authorised you to take Direct Debits in your 'Customers' tab. Reminding a customer who hasn't yet authorised you is easy.

Click onto the 'Customers' tab and then select the 'Pending' customers filter. These are customers who haven't yet authorised Direct Debit payments to you.

Clicking on the 'i' symbol on the left hand customer will open a popover with some actions you can perform on this pending customer. You can then click the 'Send reminder' link, which will send a reminder email to your customer.

Receive your money

We need your bank details so that we can make payouts to you. You can add or amend these details at any time from the 'Settings' screens.

Your bank details are displayed in the 'Bank accounts' section. To update your address or account holder name, click into that account. To add a new account click the 'Add a new bank account' button. In either case we'll require you to enter your password.

When viewing your bank details, the account we payout to will be shown as your 'Active account'. To switch between accounts just click 'Use this account'.
