Business development

We are looking for someone to help support our growing sales & marketing efforts. You'll work on picking high-potential new industries to target, identifying & researching new prospects and preparing pitches.

You'll have the opportunity to work directly with founders of the company to drive the growth of the business. As the team grows, there will be scope to develop & expand your role.

On a day-to-day basis, you will be:

  • Analysing our existing high-value clients to identify & rank potential new verticals for GoCardless
  • Identifying shortlists of high-potential prospects within each vertical & researching the key decision-makers at each
  • Researching potential leads, preparing focussed pitch-decks & supporting the founders and sales teams in pitch meetings

Requirements:

  • Strong work ethic and drive
  • Great oral & written communication skills
  • Smart with strong analytical & research skills

Bonus points for:

  • Tech savvy

Salary in the range of £20-30,000 per year. This role would ideally suit a recent graduate with 0-3 years’ professional experience
