Sales manager

Play a key role in growing one of London’s most exciting tech start-ups.

GoCardless is growing fast and we are looking for an organised, metrics-driven and inspirational sales manager to run our close-knit sales team and help accelerate our growth.

Every day you will be:

  • Measuring and managing performance to ensure we hit activity and output based KPIs individually and as a team
  • Implementing and optimising processes to improve performance
  • Develop a sales strategy including which verticals to target, how and when

You will work with an incredibly talented team including working closely with one of the founders

There’s very little hierarchy, a common hunger to learn, lots of freedom and all the responsibility you want to take. There’s also a great office atmosphere, good coffee, tons of fruit and juice, Friday beers and regular dinners out.

The skills you need to be great at this role:

  • Proven track-record in sales, with a deep understanding of best practise sales processes
  • Organisation and attention to detail
  • Strong work ethic and drive; setting demanding standards for yourself and those around you

Bonus points for previous experience in the technology space or the payments industry.

Competitive base salary (negotiable depending on experience), plus performance-related bonuses
