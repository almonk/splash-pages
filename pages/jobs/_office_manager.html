Office manager

Keep our team and office running smoothly.

GoCardless is one of London’s most exciting tech start-ups. We’re a team of 30 and growing fast. We need someone proactive, organised and friendly to take care of our day-to-day administration, bring order to our working space and keep the team happy.

You’ll be responsible for:

  • The day-to-day smooth running of a busy office, from arranging repairs to buying stationery, equipment and snacks
  • Making sure invoices and expenses are paid on time
  • Welcoming visitors, working with external suppliers and distributing post
  • Organising team events, from monthly dinners to away days
  • Providing administrative support
  • All sorts of miscellaneous tasks from helping us move office to Apple store appointments and watering the plants

This is a varied, hands-on role for someone who’s keen to go the extra mile to support our fast-growing team and will take on any task, big or small.

We’re looking for someone who is:

  • Calm in a crisis and can bring order to chaos
  • Diligent and trustworthy
  • Proactive
  • Friendly and positive

If that sounds like you, email milz@gocardless.com. Please include a great covering letter, your expected salary and when you’d be able to start.
