GoCardless helped them…

  • Reduce debtor days
  • Set up customer payments in less than a minute

Challenge

“We were wasting administrative time collecting payments”

<%= link_to "Zen Systems", "http://www.zensystems.co.uk",:rel => 'nofollow' %> offer a range of network and telecommunication solutions across the UK. With customers’ VoIP fees varying on a monthly basis, and wholesale costs attached to each, Zen Systems needed a flexible but reliable way of collecting invoices.

“When you start getting large volumes of customers, there is an associated cost of sale with those services,” says Adam Howard-Dobson, Services Director. With some customers taking up to 3 months to pay, the uncertainty was damaging the business and we were wasting administrative time collecting payments.

Solution

“With GoCardless, setting up customers took less than a minute”

“We knew that Direct Debit would put us in control of our cash flow. With GoCardless, setting up customers took less than a minute,” explains Adam.

Zen Systems now require all VoIP customers to sign up with GoCardless.

Benefits

“Admin time spent collecting payments has all but disappeared”

Since switching to GoCardless, Zen Systems have been able to easily collect fees varying on a monthly basis while reducing admin time.

“Thanks to GoCardless, invoices are now paid on time, meaning the admin time spent collecting payments has all but disappeared and cash flow issues are a thing of the past.”
