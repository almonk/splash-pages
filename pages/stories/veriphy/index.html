Veriphy eliminated admin time with
GoCardless’ simple online system

“Offering Direct Debit to our clients has been good for them, and allowed us to save time and make our customers happy.”

George Ford, Director, Veriphy

GoCardless helped them…

  • Save two days of management time every month
  • Focus on growing the business
  • Offer customers Direct Debit as a payment option

Challenge

“Managing payments for all our customers was a nightmare”

Veriphy provide anti-money laundering checks, company checks and other forms of verification to thousands of businesses. Before using GoCardless, customers were making payments using standing order and cheque.

“Managing payments for all our customers was a nightmare. It was taking us 2 full days a month in admin time to chase customers and process payments”, explained Director George Ford.

Solution

“GoCardless was the low cost Direct Debit solution we needed”

“Our customers wanted to pay by Direct Debit because remembering to write cheques and change standing orders was a hassle. However, until we found GoCardless, it was just too expensive and too much hassle to make the switch. GoCardless was the low cost Direct Debit solution we needed,” says George.

Veriphy now ask all their customers to sign up using GoCardless.

Benefits

“Customers don’t need to worry about making payments any more”

Since using GoCardless, George and his staff no longer need to spend two days a month on chasing customers and processing payments.

“Getting customers set up on GoCardless has eliminated the admin time, allowing us to focus on growing the business. Customers don’t need to worry about making payments any more, which helps both them and us”.
